Methodology and Scoring Transparency

This page documents the current model. It is a research prototype and not a diagnostic or medical device output.

Score range 0 to 100
EMA smoothing \(\alpha = 0.3\)
EEG/BCI artifact and biomarker contribution Pending integration

Implementation Equations

\[ B=\mathrm{mean}(p(t)),\ t\in[t_{on}-t_{base},t_{on}],\quad P_{6}=\mathrm{mean}(p(t)),\ t\in[t_{off}+5,t_{off}+7],\quad P_{30}=\mathrm{mean}(p(t)),\ t\in[t_{off}+25,t_{off}+35] \]

\[ \mathrm{PIPR6}=B-P_{6},\quad \mathrm{PIPR30}=B-P_{30} \]

\[ S_{pupil}=100\cdot\mathrm{clip}\left(\frac{\max(0,B-L_{min})-0.2}{2.0},0,1\right) \]

where \(L_{min}\) is the minimum valid pupil value during LIGHT ON.

\[ S_{recovery}=100\cdot\mathrm{clip}\left(\frac{\mathrm{PIPR30}}{1.2},0,1\right) \]

\[ S_{gaze\_stability}=100\cdot\left(1-\mathrm{clip}\left(\frac{J_{rms}}{0.08},0,1\right)\right) \]

\[ S_{session}= \frac{\sum_i w_i S_i}{\sum_i w_i} \quad\text{for available components only, with}\quad (w_{pupil},w_{recovery},w_{gaze})=(0.65,0.25,0.10) \]

\[ EMA_t=\alpha\cdot S_{session,t}+(1-\alpha)\cdot EMA_{t-1},\quad \alpha=0.3 \]

Scores are bounded to \([0,100]\) before storage, so EMA remains bounded as a convex combination.

\(J_{rms}\) is RMS gaze jitter computed from frame-to-frame displacement after normalizing gaze coordinates to \([0,1]\times[0,1]\).

Interpretation Boundaries

1. The gaze-stability term uses eye jitter only and is intentionally low-weight.

2. Lower jitter can indicate relaxation, steadier fixation, or attention; it is not a pure concentration measure.

3. EEG/BCI integration is planned primarily for artifact rejection and additional biomarkers, not only attention inference.

4. PIPR windows and score scaling are engineering heuristics for trend tracking in this prototype, not normative clinical thresholds.

5. Circadian engagement here is supportive telemetry and should not be used as a diagnostic output.

Methodology is transparent and versioned in code.
